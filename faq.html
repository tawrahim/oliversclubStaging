Returns & Refunds

  • CAN I RETURN MY PURCHASE?

    Unfortunately we do not accept any returns or issue refunds for any car, car parts, machineries, bikes, motorcycles.

    For other merchandize, returns and refund is based upon the merchant policy. We recommend that you purchase warranty and insurance for all your item purchased.

    If you receive a merchandize (mailed item) that is damaged, you must sign for the shipment as "received damaged". The shipping company will then be responsible for the damaged part.

    After receiving and signing for the part, contact our representative, and we'll assist you with the claim and replacement process. But we do not guarantee that your request will be granted and awarded refund.

Shipping & Delivery

  • WHEN CAN I EXPECT DELIVERY OF MY ORDER?

    Items bought on or before the 1st day of the month will be shipped on the 15th of the same month, Items paid for on or before the 15th of each month will be shipped on the last day of the month.

    You can expect most packages to be delivered within 10-14 business days from either the 15th or the last day of the month.

    Orders are processed within 24 business hours after full payment has been received, and items will leave our facilities as stated above. You will receive a confirmation letter.

    Tracking information is provided via email 24 to 48 hours after your item has been shipped. Please be sure you have provided a valid email address, and check your spam or "junk" folder as communications from jajtlist.com may not make it past your filter, depending on your settings.

    If after 14 business days you haven't received your order, please contact our shipping department at shiipingitems@jajtlist.com

    You can expect delivery of your car via ro-ro within 6 – 12 weeks depending on the cargo lines you used.

    You can expect delivery of your car and parts via container within 8-24 weeks depending on the cargo line you used.

    Remember that: all bookings of car shipment are subject to available space. All dates are estimates and subject to change without notice.

  • WILL THERE BE A HANDLING FEE FOR MY ORDER?

    Yes. The handling fee covers packaging materials needed to provide the safe arrival of your purchase. It is typically included with the shipping charge.

  • CAN I ARRANGE MY OWN SHIPPING?

    No. We have very carefully selected commercial carriers in order to provide the best coverage and care for your products. We therefore do not allow anyone to arrange for their own shipping. By letting us package items, select the best shipping method, and coordinate freight pick-ups, you are guaranteed the most secure and efficient course at the best possible price.

  • WHAT METHODS OF SHIPPING DOES JAJTLIST.COM USE?

    We ship all merchandize (clothes, shoes, books, necklaces, ect) via USPS, UPS, FEDEX., DHL. We can only ship cars that runs and drives and are intact via ro-ro service.

    We can also ship cars, engines, bumpers, car parts, liquid or large or oversized items (motors, transmissions, seats, wheels, bumpers and tires) use container services.
